How-To Guide on Soccer Hours Android App




How to Sign up to SoccerHours
Tap the Sign up link


This screen will open


Add your info and click submit to create an user account on SoccerHour



How to Log in to SoccerHours
Open the app, this screen will open


Enter your credentials and tap LOGIN to log into the app



How to start a new event
after logging into the app, tap the "Start Event" button on the home page


This screen will open


Enter the details of the event to be organized and tap the "Join Game" button


The event has been successfully created!



How to see the games joined by you
On the home screen, tap the "Joined Games" button


Your joined games will appear here



How to search games in an area
On the home screen, tap the "Search Games" button


This screen will open


Enter the zipcode of the area where you want to search games and tap "Search Games"


This screen shows all the events in the searched area


In the search results, tap on any game to see its details, tap join to join the game.
