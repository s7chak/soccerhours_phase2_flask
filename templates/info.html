The Game!

Soccer is getting increasingly popular in the US and especially in the city of Austin. This is reflected by the unprecedented interest in watching and playing the beautiful game. Also the recent success and popularity of the US National Womens Team has illustrates that. This rise in popularity of football has given new life to the sport, which has resulted in the expansion of the largest soccer league in the States to Austin, with Austin FC starting operations from next year. With this interest in the sport more and more people want to play it. But they do not know where the games are? what are the venues one could play soccer at? Where do you find other people in your area who want to play soccer? This is where SoccerHours comes in! SoccerHours provides one stop solution to all soccer games needs. Users can create events, join existing games, find games in their area. And they can do all this from their android smartphone!
